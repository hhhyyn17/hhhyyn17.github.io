*** 1. printf문-화면 출력
---
출력문
~~~C
int main()
{
  
  printf("May I help ");
  printf("you? ");
  printf("도와 ");
  printf("드릴까요? ");
  printf("What is your hobby? 취미가");
  printf(" 무엇입니까?");
  return 0;

  } 
~~~

|출력결과|
May I help you? 도와 드릴까요? What is your hobby? 취미가 무엇입니까?
